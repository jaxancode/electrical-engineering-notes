# Ohm’s Law (GATE Level)

## Concept
Ohm’s Law states that current is directly proportional to voltage across a conductor when temperature is constant.

## Mathematical Form
V = IR

## Key Insight
- Linear relationship between V and I
- Slope of V-I graph = Resistance

## Graph
Straight line passing through origin

## Trick
If graph is not linear → Ohm’s law not applicable

## Numerical
Given:
R = 5Ω, V = 10V  
Find I:

I = V/R = 10/5 = 2A

## GATE Level Question
A resistor follows V = 2I². Is it Ohmic?

Answer: No (non-linear relation)
